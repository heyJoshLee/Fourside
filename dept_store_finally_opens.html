Department Store Finally Opens

A new department store has opened in Fourside. The megastore provides Foursidians multiple floors of goods. Shops in The Department Store sell items such as: toys, sporting goods, baked goods and condiments. The department store was scheduled to open at the beginning of the year, but setback caused multiple delays. When asked about the delays a company spokesperson replied, "We were hoping to open sooner, but our lighting system kept breaking."
