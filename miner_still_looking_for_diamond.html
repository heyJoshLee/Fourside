Miner Still Looking For Diamond

The dig site in neighboring Dusty Dunes is finally up and running. The dig site started off slow, but it is now operating fully. The main miner on the project told us, “Everything was going really slow. Real slow. I thought my hole was good, but it wasn’t. Bad hole. One day some kid came by and really helped me out. He invested in my operation and even gave me something to eat. I told the kid I’d give him me first find.”

The dig team expects to find something as soon as the wild animals in the hole are tamed.
