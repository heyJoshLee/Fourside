More Dinosaurs Coming to Town

The Fourside Dinosaur Museum prepares to show two new exhibits this weekend, including new dinosaur skeletons and other related exhibits. These exhibits were scheduled to open last summer, but outside factors lead to the delay. Mr. Fork, the museum's spokesperson said "My business partner, Mr. Spoon just took way too long. He was supposed to be helping me, but he always got lost in one of the storage rooms. And to make matters worse, he always came back from the storage room smelling like a garbage dump."

The Dinosaur Museum opens this weekend and charges $6.00 person.
